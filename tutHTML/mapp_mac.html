While in the Finder app, click on the (1.) Go tab and select the (2.) Connect to Server... option

In the (3.) Server Address textbox, enter smb://ibscrs.colorado.edu/data. Optionally, you can click the "+" button to the right and add the server to your favorites

The username and password are your Identikey username and Password

Once your credentials are accepted, the "data" drive will be mapped to your Mac. To make it visible on your desktop, go to your (6.) Finder app and click on (7.) Preferences...

In the Preferences menu, make sure the (8.) Connected servers checkbox is checked

On your desktop, you should see the "data" drive
